A note from our founder

Why I started Southwest Recovery Services.

Three decades ago I set out to prove a collection agency could get you paid without burning the customer relationships you spent years building. Here’s the story — in about thirty seconds.

Have you ever wondered why the collection agency industry has such a bad reputation?

I asked myself that same question back in 1994. I knew there had to be a better way to help business owners recover from overdue customers — one that didn’t rely on the same tired playbook of aggressive calls and empty threats.

Fast forward three decades, and what started as one desk has grown into Southwest Recovery Services — a national, family-owned receivables recovery partner. But one founding principle hasn’t changed: treating people with respect opens the door to a real conversation — and a real conversation is the only thing that ever actually solves a past-due account.

Our approach is simple. We show up kind-hearted, even-toned, and empathetic — every call, every time. It’s how we forge a path forward that gets you paid without burning the customer relationship you’ve worked to build.

That’s the difference between us and the old mindset of the rude bill collector. We’re solution-driven, not confrontation-driven — and it’s why our clients keep their customers even after we’ve closed the account.

— Steven Dietz, Founder & CEO
